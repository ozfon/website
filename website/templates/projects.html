Pressurized Oxy-Coal Reactor

I completed and tested BYU's Pressurized Oxy-Coal Reactor. I worked on this reactor from 2019-2021 and wrote my thesis on this reactor. This work inlcuded wiring the logic controller, pressure testing the pressure vessel, curring the refractory with a natural gas flame, feeding coal for the first time, and more.

Shadowgraph Imaging

For a class project in my graduate level compressible flow class I produced images of gas flow using a technique called shadowgraph imaging. The picture shows the hot gas from a lighter refracting the light differently then the cooler ambient air. I was also able to visualize shockwaves from the nozzle of a compressed air gun. This method enables the differnces in densities of a fluid to be visiable to the human eye or cameras.
